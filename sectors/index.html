Sectors

The role stays
consistent. The application
changes.

I advise across six sectors: CPG, hospitality, wellness studios, founder, talent, and athlete ventures, apparel, and longevity and beauty. The problems are the same regardless of category. Brand positioning that is not doing enough work. A commercial model that has not caught up with the ambition. A go-to-market plan built on assumptions. The industry changes. The diagnosis rarely does.

CPG

Food, beverage, supplements, wellness products.

For emerging and growth-stage CPG brands in food, beverage, supplements, and wellness products that need sharper brand strategy, commercial structure, and a clearer route to market.

Best fit
Emerging brands · Launch-stage businesses · Growth-stage operators
Typical tensions
Weak positioning · Margin and channel confusion · Launches without enough commercial structure behind them

Pricing, forecasting, KPI review, and financial modeling

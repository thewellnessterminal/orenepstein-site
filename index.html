Oren Epstein is a brand and business advisor working with founders and operators across consumer, wellness, hospitality, and experience-led businesses. Advisory
The work

I come in when the thinking behind the business needs to get sharper.

Most businesses do not break because the ambition is too small. They break because the foundation underneath the ambition is not strong enough.

Sometimes the brand sounds better than the actual experience. Sometimes the offer is too hard to understand. Sometimes the business is growing, but the model behind it does not hold. That is usually where money gets wasted, momentum slows, and the next stage gets harder than it should be.

I bring outside judgment, real operating experience, and the directness to call what is not working. I founded and scaled a nationally distributed CPG brand, and now advise businesses that want to get clearer, stronger, and more commercially sound before the next move. When execution needs specialists, I bring in the right people through The Better Advisors.

Sectors

Industry-agnostic.
Pattern-aware.

The skill is judgment, not a vertical. A supplement brand, a members club, and a medspa all face the same core questions: who are we for, what do we actually offer, and how do we build a business behind it that holds under pressure. I work across categories because the patterns are more alike than they are different.

Retail strategy for brands built to sell through competitive, margin-sensitive channels. Native territory—where the advisory practice started.

Best fit

Brands with initial traction, entering retail expansion or launching a new product vertical. Businesses preparing investor materials or rethinking pricing and positioning before the next phase.

Tensions I help resolve

Hospitality: Hotels, Resorts, Wellness + Experience

Guest-facing businesses where brand, experience, and commercial model have to work as one system. From boutique hotels and wellness resorts to private clubs and destination concepts — including properties where wellness is becoming the operating identity, not a line item on the spa menu.

Best fit

Hospitality concepts in development or pre-launch. Existing properties repositioning for a different guest segment, wellness integration, or higher market tier. Resorts embedding wellness into brand identity, guest journey, and revenue architecture. Properties preparing for Forbes, WITT certification, or investor-grade standards.

Tensions I help resolve

"We have a beautiful property but the concept does not hold together." · "We want wellness to be our identity but it still lives in the spa." · "We have five vendor partnerships and no cohesive guest experience." · "Our brand says luxury but the delivery does not pass the test." · "We need a wellness strategy that shows up in the P&L, not just the marketing."

Collaboration strategy — brand partnerships that strengthen, not dilute
Standards readiness: Forbes, WITT, and category benchmarks
Business Infrastructure

Partnership deal structure and margin protection
Operations review, hiring, and vendor coordination

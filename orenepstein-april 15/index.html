Oren Epstein is a brand and business advisor working with consumer, wellness, hospitality, and experience-driven businesses across North America. The work happens at moments of build, refinement, or transition - and the standard is whether the most discerning person in the room would be impressed.

The work

I come in when a business needs a strategic advisor to sharpen the thinking behind what they are building.

The problems are usually the same across categories. Brand positioning that is doing too little. A commercial model that has not caught up with the ambition. A guest or customer experience that does not hold up under the scrutiny of the most discerning evaluators in the category.

I bring outside judgment, real operating experience (including founding and scaling a nationally distributed CPG brand), and the directness to call what is not working. I know what best-in-class looks like across consumer, wellness, and hospitality - and I make sure the businesses I work with can meet that bar, not just claim it. When execution requires specialists, I pull in the right people. The advisory platform is The Better Advisors.

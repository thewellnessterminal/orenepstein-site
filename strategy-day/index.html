Signature offer

The Strategy Day.

The Strategy Day is a private brand strategy session created by Oren Epstein for founders, operators, and talent building consumer, wellness, hospitality, and lifestyle ventures who need strategic clarity before their next move. A full-day working session for businesses facing important decisions, too many moving parts, or a concept that needs sharper direction.

Most businesses have the information. What they need is an outside advisor who can sort it, call what matters, and make the path forward obvious. That is what the Strategy Day is for.

How it works
Before
Session prep
Review of your existing materials, notes, and current priorities
Clarify the decisions that matter most before we meet
Shape the session around the business stage and immediate need
During
The working session
A focused session built around the highest-leverage questions
Live advisory across concept, model, growth, and execution
Clear direction on what to tighten, sequence, or move next
After
Written output
Written summary with key takeaways and recommendations
Prioritized next steps and roadmap direction
Optional follow-on advisory support where needed
Who it is for

You already know something is off.

The Strategy Day is not for businesses with no direction. And not for businesses that have everything figured out. It is for the ones in between, where the stakes are real and the next move matters.

The founder three weeks from launch
Everything is in motion but nothing feels locked. Pricing is a guess. The positioning deck keeps changing. You need someone outside the building to tell you what is actually ready and what is not.
The operator who grew faster than the model
Revenue is up but margins are unclear. The team is busy but the priorities feel scattered. You need someone to look at the whole picture and name what to fix first.
The brand that looks great but is not converting
The visuals are sharp, the product is solid, but the business behind it is not holding. Something in the offer, the positioning, or the channel strategy is leaking and you cannot tell where.
The supplement brand with a finished product and no commercial plan
The formula is done. The packaging looks right. But you have no retail strategy, no pricing model, no channel plan, and no idea how the industry actually works. You need someone who has built and sold a CPG brand to walk you through every step you have not thought of yet.
The person with a strong idea and capital but no structure yet
You know what you want to build. You do not know the right order to build it in, what to spend on first, or whether the concept holds commercially. You need a day to find out.
The talent or athlete with a strong idea and no operating structure
You have the name, the audience, and the relationships. What you do not have is a clear business model, the right partners, or someone pressure-testing whether the venture is commercially ready. You need a day with someone who builds businesses, not just brands.
What happens after

The day ends. The work continues.

The Strategy Day is not a one-off. It is how most of my longer advisory relationships and network introductions begin. Once I understand the business, I know exactly who you need to talk to and what should happen next.

1
Written roadmap. A summary of what we covered, the key recommendations, and a prioritized sequence of next steps. Delivered within a few days of the session.
2
Introductions to my network. If you need a designer, PR agency, financial partner, architect, co-packer, or digital team, I make the connection directly. Not a list. A warm intro to the right person for your specific situation.
3
Deeper strategic work. For businesses that need more than a day, the Strategy Day often leads into a Brand and Business Architecture engagement or an ongoing advisory retainer. The day becomes the diagnostic. The architecture becomes the plan.

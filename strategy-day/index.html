Signature offer

The Strategy Day.

A private strategy session for founders and operators who need clarity fast. Built for businesses facing important decisions, too many moving parts, or a brand concept that needs sharper direction before the next move.

This is not a coaching session. It is a working day: structured, direct, and designed to leave with concrete next steps. The goal is to compress months of circling into a single, focused engagement.

How the day works
Before
Intake + Preparation
Pre-session questionnaire covering business context, goals, and open questions
Review of relevant materials: decks, financials, brand assets, competitive context
Initial research and preparation on my end so we start sharp
During
The Working Day
3-4 hours of focused, structured conversation (virtual or in-person)
Deep diagnosis of where you are and what is actually broken or missing
Real-time pressure-testing, strategic direction, and problem-solving
After
Output + Follow-Up
Written summary of strategic direction, recommendations, and next steps
Prioritized action list with sequencing and resource guidance
30-day follow-up call to pressure-test progress and adjust course

Who this is for.

The Strategy Day is designed for a specific kind of founder or operator. You are past the early scrappy phase. You have traction, capital, or both. You are stuck on something specific and want outside pressure to get unstuck.

The founder preparing for a fundraise
"I need to sharpen the story, the model, and the deck before I go out."
The operator stuck at a plateau
"We've grown but I don't know what lever to pull next."
The brand that has lost clarity
"We know what we do but we can't explain why it matters."
The business facing a big decision
"We have options but no framework to choose between them."
The leader who needs outside perspective
"I've been too close to this for too long. I need someone who will tell me what I'm missing."

What happens after.

The Strategy Day is a standalone engagement. But many clients continue into longer advisory work once they see how the thinking translates to execution.

1
Some clients are done. They get the clarity they needed, implement the recommendations, and move forward independently. That is a success.
2
Some clients want ongoing pressure-testing. A monthly or quarterly check-in to stay on track and adjust course as things evolve.
3
Some clients need deeper work. Brand architecture, financial modeling, go-to-market strategy, or a structured multi-month engagement. The Strategy Day becomes the diagnosis; the advisory work becomes the treatment.
